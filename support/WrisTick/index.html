WrisTick — Support

Metronome and tuner for watchOS.
Stay in time on your wrist. Metronome (sound/light/haptic) with Tap Tempo and drag BPM. Separate voices/volumes for Beats/Subdivisions. Tuner: A4 and sharp/flat.

Quick answers

  • Metronome: Open WrisTick — the main screen is the metronome.
  • Tuner: Long‑press TAP TEMPO to open the tuner.
  • Sound settings: Long‑press the speaker icon.
  • Flash settings: Long‑press the light‑bulb icon.
  • Haptic settings: Long‑press the hand icon.
