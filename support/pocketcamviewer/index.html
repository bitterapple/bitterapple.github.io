Quick answers

  • How do I start? Open the app on two devices. Set one to Camera, the other to Viewer.
  • The Viewer can’t find the Camera. Make sure both devices are on the same Wi-Fi network and the Camera app stays in the foreground.
  • Video looks rotated. Try rotating the Camera device and re-starting streaming.
  • Video repeats or skips. This can happen with unstable Wi-Fi. Move closer to the router or reduce other network load.
  • No audio? Check microphone permission on the Camera device (Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone).

Turn one iPhone/iPad into a Camera, and watch it from another device as a Viewer.
Prepare two or more devices signed in with the same Apple ID.

← All apps Contact support

Quick start

  1. Sign in to the same Apple ID on both devices.
  2. Open PocketCam Viewer on both devices.
  3. On Device A, select Camera.
  4. On Device B, select Viewer and choose the available camera.

Troubleshooting

The Viewer can’t find the Camera.

Confirm both devices are signed in with the same Apple ID. If your workflow uses local discovery, also make sure they are on the same Wi‑Fi network and keep the Camera device awake.

Video freezes, repeats, or skips.

This is usually network instability. Move closer to your router, reduce other network load, and avoid switching networks during a session.

Video looks rotated.

Try rotating the Camera device and restarting the stream.

No audio.

On the Camera device, allow microphone access: Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone.
