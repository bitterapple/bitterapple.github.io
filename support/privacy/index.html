Summary

My apps are designed to work without ads or third‑party tracking. I do not sell personal information.

  • No third‑party analytics or advertising SDKs are used.
  • Some features may use Apple services (such as iCloud / CloudKit) when you enable them.
  • If you contact support or submit feedback, I will use your message only to respond and improve the app.

Information I collect

A) Support email

If you email support, I receive the information you choose to include (for example your email address and message content).

B) In‑app feedback (when available)

Some apps may provide an in‑app feedback form. If you send feedback, I may receive: your message, app version/build, device model, OS version, locale, and a timestamp.

C) iCloud / CloudKit (optional)

Some apps can use iCloud / CloudKit to sync settings or content across your devices. This is optional and depends on the app and your iCloud settings. Data stored in iCloud is associated with your Apple ID and handled by Apple.

I do not collect precise location data, contacts, or microphone recordings for advertising. Permissions (camera/microphone/files) are used only for core app features when you choose to enable them.

How I use information

  • To provide app functionality you request (for example syncing via iCloud if enabled).
  • To respond to support requests and troubleshoot issues.
  • To improve stability and fix bugs.

Sharing

I do not sell personal information. I do not share personal information with third parties for advertising. If you use iCloud/CloudKit features, Apple processes and stores that data according to your Apple account settings and Apple’s policies.

Data retention

Support emails and feedback messages may be kept as long as needed to respond, debug, and improve the apps. iCloud/CloudKit data is retained according to your iCloud settings and can typically be removed by deleting the app’s iCloud data in your device settings.

Your choices

  • You can choose not to send feedback or email support.
  • You can disable iCloud for an app in iOS/iPadOS/macOS settings.
  • You can revoke permissions (camera/microphone/files) at any time in system settings.

Changes

This policy may be updated as the apps evolve. The “Last updated” date will reflect changes.
