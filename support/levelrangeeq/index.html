Level Range EQ

Version 1.2.0 — Coming soon (signing in progress).

Released / uploaded: 2026-01-09

Level Range EQ is an EQ plug-in that lets you apply different EQ curves to different level ranges of the signal — so quiet parts, mid-level parts, and loud parts can each be shaped differently.

What it does

  • Apply different EQ depending on the signal’s level.
  • Three level ranges: Low, Mid, High.
  • Each range has its own EQ curve, so you can shape whispers / normal / peaks differently.

Typical use cases

Vocals

Keep quiet phrases warm, keep normal phrases clear, and tame harsh peaks — without crushing everything into one curve.

Dialogue / VO

Different tonal balance for low-level room tone vs spoken words vs loud consonants — useful for consistent intelligibility.

Instruments

Shape sustain and transients differently (e.g., brighten attacks while keeping body smooth).
